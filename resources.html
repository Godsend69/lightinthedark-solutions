Letters of Light

A Newsletter Unlike Any Other

No weekly updates. No content marketing. No tips for optimizing your morning routine. Letters of Light is sent when I have something genuinely worth saying — which averages twice a month.

Each letter is a short essay, a reflection, or a direct piece of guidance drawn from the work I am doing — with myself and with the people I advise. Honest. Personal. Often challenging.

Subscribers also receive the Sovereignty Workbook immediately upon signing up — and early access to all new guides and programs.

Recommended Reading

Books That Changed the Work

These are books I return to. Books that have shaped how I think about human sovereignty, freedom, and the nature of the dark.

M

Man's Search for Meaning

Viktor Frankl

The book that proved will can survive anything. The origin of everything I believe about human sovereignty.

R

The Republic

Plato

The cave allegory remains the most precise description of the human condition ever written. Still true. Still urgent.

D

Discipline and Punish

Michel Foucault

The architecture of obedience — mapped with terrifying precision. Read this to understand what you are actually living inside.

N

Thus Spoke Zarathustra

Nietzsche

The challenge to become who you actually are — not what comfort, habit, and fear have shaped you to be.
